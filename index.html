Customer Personality Analysis Using SQL & PowerBI

Customer personality analysis helps a business to modify its product based on its target customers from different types of customer segments. For example, instead of spending money to market a new product to every customer in the company’s database, a company can analyze which customer segment is most likely to buy the product and then market the product only on that particular segment.
In this project SQL was used to perform clustering to summarize customer segments and Power BI was used to create visualization.

Job Platform Popularity Over Time in Nigeria Using SQL & Tableau

A lack of job opportunities causes high poverty levels, regional inequality, and social and political unrest. Fortunately, hundreds of job sites in Nigeria use the internet to keep track of the most recent job vacancies, Career Opportunities for Nigerians, and other crucial career information for the average Nigerian or expatriate job seeker.
This project used SQL to query data gotten from google trend in other to find the job platform popularity over time in nigeria. Additionally, Tableau was used to create visualization of the results.
